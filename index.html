Information
Cells start out as either dead or alive (click to toggle).
As the 'generations' progress, each cells fate is determined by its neighbors.

1) Any live cell with less than 2 neighbors dies (underpopulation)
2) Any live cell with more than 3 neighbors dies (overcrowding)
4) Any dead cell with 3 neighbors comes to life (reproduction)
